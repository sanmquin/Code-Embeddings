These functions are responsible for the final rendering and modification of the grid state. Key operations include:
- **Shape Rendering**: Drawing spirals, lines, and rectangles based on calculated boundaries or markers.
- **Flood & Fill**: Filling specific areas with colors, often determined by contextual logic (like rectangle dimensions).
- **Conditional Updates**: Transforming specific cell values (e.g., beam projection, color replacement) based on rules.
- **Marker Application**: Updating the grid with identified marker values.
