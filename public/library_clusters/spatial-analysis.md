This cluster focuses on analyzing the 2D grid to identify structural elements and spatial relationships. It includes functions for:
- **Object Detection**: Finding connected components (BFS/DFS), blobs of color, or specific coordinate sets.
- **Boundary Analysis**: Calculating bounding boxes, finding barriers (lines), and determining thresholds for signal vs. noise.
- **Pattern Extraction**: Normalizing shapes into canonical patterns and identifying recurring bands or tiles.
- **Geometric Properties**: Locating specific coordinates and analyzing surrounded or isolated pixels.
