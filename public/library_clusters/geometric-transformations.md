These functions handle fundamental changes to the grid's structure and layout. They provide capabilities for:
- **Scaling & Replication**: Adjusting grid dimensions and repeating rows or patterns.
- **Symmetry Operations**: Flipping and reversing sections of the grid.
- **Grid Setup**: Creating empty workspaces and deep-cloning existing grids to prevent unintended mutations.
- **Sub-grid Extraction**: Isolating specific tiles or rectangular regions for further processing.
